Submission Instructions

All papers must represent original and unpublished work that is not currently under review. Papers will be evaluated according to their significance, originality, technical content, style, clarity, and relevance to the workshop.

We welcome the following types of contributions:

  • Full research papers (up to 12 pages)
  • Short research papers (up to 6 pages)
  • Poster papers (up to 4 pages)
  • Demo papers (up to 4 pages)

All submissions must be written in English and formatted according to LNCS instructions for authors.

Papers are to be submitted through the Easychair Conference Management System.

Important Dates

  • Paper submission deadline: Tuesday, March 1
  • Notifications to authors: Friday, April 1
  • Workshop day: 29 or 30 May 2016 - To be confirmed
